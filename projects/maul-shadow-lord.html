Maul:
Shadow Lord

BRIEF

This was an exploration of title treatments for Maul: Shadow Lord, an animated series centered on Darth Maul and building on the legacy established by previous Star Wars animated projects.

The goal was to create something bold, gritty, and unapologetically in-your-face, leaning into the intensity and presence of the character. The designs pull from Maul's visual language, incorporating elements like his tattoo markings, Sith wardrobe, and the stylized rendering approach of the series itself.
