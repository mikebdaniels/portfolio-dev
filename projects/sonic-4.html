BRIEF

A title reveal for Sonic the Hedgehog 4 — charging the iconic lockup with raw electricity. Neon filaments arc and snap into place as the film's title ignites out of the dark.

Designed and animated at Aspect, with Houdini-driven lightning and FX. The goal was energy you could feel through the screen: fast, luminous, and unmistakably Sonic.
